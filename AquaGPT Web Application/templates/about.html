About AquaGPT

Our Mission

AquaGPT is dedicated to revolutionizing aquaculture management through artificial intelligence. We aim to provide fish farmers and aquaculture professionals with instant access to expert knowledge and disease diagnosis capabilities.

Key Features

Disease Detection

Using state-of-the-art AI models (MobileNetV2, DenseNet121, and VGG16), we provide accurate fish disease diagnosis through image analysis.

Expert Knowledge Base

Access comprehensive information about aquaculture management, best practices, and solutions to common problems.

Real-time Assistance

Get immediate answers to your questions about fish farming, disease management, and aquaculture optimization.

Technology Stack

  • Advanced Language Models for natural conversation
  • Deep Learning models for image analysis
  • Real-time processing for instant responses
  • Secure and scalable cloud infrastructure
